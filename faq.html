Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about accounts, job posting, safety, and site usage.

Do I need an account to browse jobs?

No. You can browse public job listings without creating an account.

Do I need an account to post a job?

Yes. You need to sign in first to create and manage job postings.

How do I post a good job listing?

Use a clear title, explain tasks in detail, include budget and timeline, and provide reliable contact information.

Can I post remote or online work?

Yes. Mark the listing as online and include a valid sample or reference link when requested.

How can I edit or remove my job post?

Sign in and use your profile page to manage jobs posted from your account.

How are jobs sorted?

Jobs can be sorted by newest, budget high-to-low, or budget low-to-high using filters on the Jobs page.

Is my data shared publicly?

Only details you place in public listings are visible to other users. Review Privacy Policy for full details.

What should I do if I find suspicious content?

Do not engage. Capture the listing details and report it through the Contact page.

Why do I not see my jobs on another device?

Listings try to load from the cloud when available, with local browser storage as a fallback. If something is missing on another device, sign in again or check your connection.

How can I contact support?

Use the Contact page to send your message and include as much detail as possible.
