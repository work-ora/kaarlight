About Kaarlight

Connecting Afghan talent with local opportunities, one job at a time.

Our Mission

Kaarlight is a simple, fast platform designed to connect Afghan freelancers, businesses, and individuals with local job opportunities. We believe in making work accessible - whether you are a small business looking to hire, a freelancer searching for your next project, or someone offering services in your community.

Why Kaarlight?

  • Quick Sign-In: Create an account in under a minute to post and manage jobs easily.
  • Local Focus: Browse opportunities across Afghan cities and connect with people in your area.
  • Direct Contact: Reach freelancers and employers directly via phone or email - no middleman.
  • Fair Pricing: You set your own rates with no hidden fees.
  • Safe and Transparent: Build trust through direct communication and clear contact details.

What You Can Do

Job Seekers

Explore freelance, short-term, and full-time opportunities across Afghanistan. Filter by category, location, and budget.

Employers

Post a job in 2 minutes. Set your budget, describe the work, and connect with qualified professionals.

Freelancers

Showcase your skills and portfolio. Build your reputation by accepting gigs and delivering quality work.

Fairness
